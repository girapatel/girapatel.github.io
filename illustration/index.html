Overview

These are a mix of personal illustrations, as well as work used for past clients. I enjoy experimenting with different ways to illustrate, but my favorite way to draw is with an Apple Pencil on Adobe Illustrator or Fresco. I sometimes use Procreate but prefer drawing in vector, so it isn't my go-to.

Tools Used

Illustrator, Fresco, Apple Pencil
