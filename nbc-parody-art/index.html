Overview

Saturday Night Live and Late Night with Seth Meyers often feature sketches that parody pop culture. Our design team makes parody movie posters, album art, and everything in-between as a fun way to complement high-performing clips on Instagram. For SNL, we have 1-4 hours to make each piece. These are some that I made.
