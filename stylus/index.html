Overview
Stylus is a literary journal that features writing and poetry from students at the University of Maryland. I redesigned their website to focus on content that students primarily use: deadlines, guidelines, and the submission portal. They didn't have any solid branding, so I designed their logo and created basic visual guidelines for typography and color. I decided to code the website from scratch because the original website wasn't responsive and there wasn't budget to use a builder or developer.

Tools
Photoshop, Illustrator, HTML, CSS, PHP, JQuery
