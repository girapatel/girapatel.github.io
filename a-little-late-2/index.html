Overview

I led digital design for the second season of A Little Late. The show is extremely different from season one, switching from a studio to a house with no audience. It is more true to Lilly Singh's personality, so we refreshed digital and social assets to feel more like "Lilly" and less like "Late Night Television," without making it a clone of her YouTube channel.

Tools Used

Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, After Effects
